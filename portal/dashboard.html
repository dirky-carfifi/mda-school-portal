School Philosophy

Monlimar Development Academy is a non-sectarian educational institution committed to providing an inclusive, high-quality learning environment that embraces diversity in all forms. The school fosters academic excellence and holistic development through a supportive and conducive atmosphere, aiming to develop resilient learners with the necessary skills, competencies, a strong nationalistic spirit, and strong moral and ethical values. The school also strives to empower an empathetic, highly competent, and adaptable teaching workforce to meet the evolving needs of Monlimarian learners.

School Mission

Monlimar Development Academy aims to create a dynamic learning environment that equips learners with 21st century skills, fostering their growth into competent and responsible citizens who make meaningful contributions to society, offering programs that inspire them to reach their full potentials.

School Vision

Monlimar Development Academy aims to be an exemplary educational institution where learners develop resiliency, respect for all, achieve global competitiveness, and contribute positively to society and the nation.

Goals and Objectives

At Monlimar Development Academy, we are committed to fostering a holistic learning environment where learners can grow academically, socially, and personally. Our goals and objectives are designed to cultivate well-rounded individuals who are prepared to face the challenges of the modern world.

Our School History

Monlimar Development Academy has a rich history of providing quality education to students in our community. Founded with a vision to create a nurturing environment that fosters both academic excellence and personal growth, our school has grown from humble beginnings to become a leading educational institution.

1990

Monlimar Development Academy was founded by a group of educators with a vision to provide quality education to the local community. The school started with just 50 students and 5 teachers.

1995

The school expanded its facilities to accommodate growing enrollment, adding new classrooms and a library. The first batch of high school graduates completed their studies.

2002

Monlimar Development Academy received accreditation from the Department of Education, recognizing the school's commitment to quality education and innovative teaching methods.

2010

The school celebrated its 20th anniversary with the opening of a new science laboratory and computer center, marking a significant step forward in STEM education.

2018

Monlimar Development Academy launched its international exchange program, partnering with schools in three different countries to provide global learning opportunities.

2023

The school completed a major renovation project, adding state-of-the-art facilities including a performing arts center and expanded sports complex.

Today, Monlimar Development Academy continues to build on its legacy of excellence, preparing students to meet the challenges of the 21st century with confidence, creativity, and compassion.
